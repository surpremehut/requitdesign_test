Shipping Policy

Last updated: January 2025 · RequitDesign, Germany

At RequitDesign, we're committed to getting your order to you quickly and safely. Read on for important information about our shipping process.

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 2-3 business days. During this time, your case is carefully packed and prepared for shipment. You'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order ships.

Shipping Carriers

We partner with trusted international shipping providers to ensure your order arrives safely:

  • Deutsche Post (Germany)
  • DHL (International)
  • DPD (International)

Shipping Rates & Delivery Times

Destination Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Cost
Germany Standard Shipping 3-5 business days Free
Europe (EU) International Standard 5-10 business days €9,99
Worldwide International Shipping 10-21 business days €19,99

Note: Delivery times are estimates and do not include customs delays or processing. Orders shipped outside the EU may be subject to customs duties and taxes.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your shipment in real-time with your carrier. Most carriers provide estimated delivery dates and delivery updates.

Free Worldwide Shipping Promotion

We occasionally offer free worldwide shipping promotions. When available, these will be clearly indicated during checkout. Take advantage of these offers to get your RequitDesign case delivered worldwide at no additional cost.

Lost or Damaged Shipments

Your satisfaction is our priority. If your order arrives damaged or doesn't arrive within the estimated timeframe:

  • Contact us immediately with photographic evidence (for damaged items)
  • Provide your order number and tracking information
  • We'll work with the carrier to resolve the issue
  • We may send a replacement or issue a refund

Customs & Duties

For international orders outside the EU, customers are responsible for any applicable customs duties or import taxes. These charges are determined by your local customs authority and are not included in the shipping cost.

Questions About Shipping?

If you have any questions about your shipment or need help tracking your order, please contact us. We're here to help.
