Section 04

Our online shop is operated via Shopify International Ltd., a third-party e-commerce platform. When you place an order or browse our shop, your data is processed on Shopify's infrastructure.

Shopify acts as a data processor on our behalf and is contractually bound to process your data only according to our instructions and in compliance with applicable data protection law.

Shopify Privacy Policy: You can find Shopify's privacy policy at shopify.com/legal/privacy. Shopify is certified under standard contractual clauses ensuring adequate protection for data transfers outside the EU/EEA.

Section 05

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to ensure basic functionality, remember your cart contents, and analyse site usage.

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These are essential for the website to function. They include session cookies, cart cookies, and security tokens. No consent is required for these.

Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics tools (e.g. Shopify Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our store. These cookies are only set with your consent.

Marketing Cookies

If you have consented, marketing cookies may be used to display relevant advertisements and measure their effectiveness across social platforms such as Instagram and TikTok.

You can withdraw your consent or manage cookies at any time via your browser settings or our cookie preference centre.

Section 08

Your Rights Under the GDPR

As a data subject, you have the following rights under Chapter III of the GDPR. You may exercise any of these rights at any time by contacting us:

Right of Access (Art. 15 GDPR)

You have the right to receive a copy of the personal data we hold about you, along with information about how it is processed.

Right to Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)

If any data we hold about you is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to have it corrected without undue delay.

Right to Erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data where it is no longer necessary for the purposes it was collected, subject to legal retention obligations.

Right to Restriction (Art. 18 GDPR)

In certain circumstances, you may request that we restrict the processing of your data rather than deleting it.

Right to Data Portability (Art. 20 GDPR)

You may request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.

Right to Object (Art. 21 GDPR)

You have the right to object to the processing of your data based on legitimate interests, including for direct marketing purposes.

Right to Withdraw Consent (Art. 7(3) GDPR)

Where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal.

Right to lodge a complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, in particular in the EU Member State of your habitual residence. In Germany, the relevant authority is the Landesbeauftragte für Datenschutz of your state of residence.

Section 09

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, alteration, or disclosure. These measures include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted between your browser and our server
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorised personnel only
  • Shopify's PCI DSS-compliant payment infrastructure
  • Regular review and updating of our security practices

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
